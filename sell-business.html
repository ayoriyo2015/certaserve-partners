Sell Your Business with Confidence

Confidential, structured, and transaction-focused support for business owners preparing for a sale, partnership transition, or exit.

Selling a business is a major decision. CertaServe Partners helps you prepare, position, and execute your sale with financial clarity and disciplined documentation. Our approach is designed to protect confidentiality, reduce deal risk, and support a smoother closing process.

What We Help You Achieve

  • Clear understanding of what you’re selling (operations, finances, assets, risks)
  • Improved deal readiness and professional documentation
  • Confidential handling and controlled information flow
  • Support through buyer questions and due diligence
  • Transaction coordination toward completion

Our Selling Process

  • 1) Discovery & Goals: understand timeline, valuation expectations, and exit priorities
  • 2) Data & Document Review: review financials, operations, contracts, and key risks
  • 3) Packaging & Positioning: prepare a structured summary for qualified buyers
  • 4) Buyer Engagement: manage information requests and support negotiations
  • 5) Due Diligence Support: organize responses and reduce delays
  • 6) Closing Support: coordinate documentation and next steps with stakeholders

What We Typically Request (Seller Checklist)

  • Last 2–3 years financial statements and/or tax returns
  • Year-to-date financials (P&L, balance sheet)
  • Customer and supplier overview (high-level; no sensitive sharing until appropriate)
  • Lease details (if applicable) and key contracts
  • Staffing overview (roles and structure)
  • Inventory, equipment, and major assets list
  • Notes on any known issues (legal, compliance, operational)

Confidentiality

We treat confidentiality as a priority. Information is shared on a controlled basis and aligned with the stage of the transaction and the readiness of the buyer.
