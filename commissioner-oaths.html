Commissioner of Oaths (By Appointment)

Quick, professional commissioning of documents for individuals and businesses in Ottawa and surrounding areas.

CertaServe Partners offers Commissioner of Oaths services by appointment. We can witness signatures, administer oaths/affirmations, and commission eligible documents in accordance with applicable requirements.

Please note: commissioning confirms the identity of the person signing and the signature. It does not confirm the truth of the contents of the document.

Common Documents We Can Commission

  • Statutory declarations
  • Affidavits (where permitted)
  • Insurance and benefit forms
  • Government and administrative forms
  • Permission letters and declarations
  • Other eligible documents (confirm with us)

What to Bring

  • Valid government-issued photo ID
  • Your document(s) unsigned (sign in front of the Commissioner)
  • Any supporting documents required by your institution
