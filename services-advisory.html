Business Advisory & Brokerage

CPA-led advisory and transaction support for entrepreneurs, buyers, investors, and organizations — from planning to closing.

CertaServe Partners supports clients with structured business advisory and brokerage-related services. Whether you are buying, selling, raising capital, or preparing for a major transaction, we provide financial clarity, disciplined documentation, and execution support.

Core Services

  • Sell a Business: exit readiness, packaging, confidentiality, buyer screening, and transaction support.
  • Buy a Business: buyer readiness, target evaluation, offer support, and transaction coordination.
  • Due Diligence & Valuation: financial due diligence, quality of earnings support, valuation analysis, and deal risk review.
  • Business Plans (SUV & Others): structured business plans for the Canadian Start-Up Visa program and other funding/strategy needs.
  • Strategic Advisory: financial modeling, performance improvement, operational clarity, and decision support.

Who We Help

  • Entrepreneurs preparing to sell or scale
  • Buyers and investors assessing opportunities
  • Immigrant entrepreneurs (Start-Up Visa pathway)
  • Lawyers and stakeholders supporting transactions
  • SMEs needing valuation, modeling, or clarity

How We Work

  • Step 1: Discovery call to understand goals, timelines, and constraints
  • Step 2: Information request and document review
  • Step 3: Analysis, deliverables, and decision support
  • Step 4: Execution support through completion
