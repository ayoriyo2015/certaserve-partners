Professional Court Filing Services

Accurate and timely court filing services for law firms, businesses, and individuals across Ottawa and surrounding areas.

CertaServe Partners provides reliable court filing support for civil, family, and small claims matters. We ensure documents are filed in accordance with applicable court rules and within required timelines.

Same-day and urgent filing options are available upon request, with confirmation of filing provided once completed.

What We File

  • Statements of Claim and Defence
  • Affidavits and Motion Materials
  • Family Law Applications and Responses
  • Small Claims Court Documents
  • Notices of Motion and Applications
  • Other court-required filings

Our Filing Process

  • Review of documents for filing completeness
  • In-person or electronic filing, as required
  • Payment of court fees (where applicable)
  • Confirmation and proof of filing provided
