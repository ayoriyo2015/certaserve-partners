Pricing

The pricing below reflects typical starting rates. Final pricing depends on scope, urgency, document volume, travel distance, and court fees (where applicable).

  • Process Serving (Standard): Starting at $95
  • Rush / Time-Specific Serving: Starting at $150
  • Court Filing: Starting at $75 + court fees
  • Legal Courier (Same-Day): Starting at $65
  • Document Management: $75 / hour or $250 / package
  • E-Discovery Support: $95 / hour
  • Commissioner of Oaths: $40 / document (by appointment)

Common Add-ons

  • Additional service attempts: $35

  • Mileage outside Ottawa core: $0.65/km
  • After-hours / weekends: Quoted

Court filing fees, where applicable, are paid by the client or reimbursed at cost.
