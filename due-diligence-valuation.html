Due Diligence & Business Valuation

Independent financial analysis to support buyers, sellers, investors, and business owners making high-stakes decisions.

CertaServe Partners provides due diligence support and valuation-focused analysis to help clients understand the true financial position, performance drivers, and key risks in a business. Whether you are buying, selling, seeking financing, or planning growth, we help you make decisions with clearer financial insight.

Due Diligence Support (Buy-Side or Sell-Side)

  • Financial statement review and normalization
  • Revenue and margin analysis (key drivers and sustainability)
  • Working capital and cash flow assessment
  • Customer concentration and contract considerations
  • Expense reasonableness and add-backs review
  • Identification of red flags and deal risks
  • Support with diligence Q&A and information requests

Valuation & Pricing Guidance

  • Valuation support for sale, purchase, or internal planning
  • Comparable-based pricing considerations (high-level)
  • Cash flow and scenario modeling
  • Deal structure considerations (high-level financial perspective)
  • Decision-ready summaries for stakeholders

What You Typically Need to Provide

  • Financial statements / tax returns (2–3 years)
  • Year-to-date financials (P&L, balance sheet)
  • Revenue detail (by product/service line if available)
  • Major contracts, lease terms, and key commitments
  • Staffing overview and payroll summary (high-level)
  • Debt schedule and major obligations

Who This Service Is For

  • Buyers and investors evaluating an acquisition
  • Sellers preparing for a sale and buyer questions
  • Business owners seeking financing or restructuring
  • SMEs needing independent analysis and clarity
