About CertaServe Partners

CertaServe Partners is an Ottawa-based, CPA-led firm providing business advisory, business brokerage, and professional legal support services.

We support entrepreneurs, investors, law firms, and organizations with transaction-focused services including due diligence, valuation, business planning (including the Canadian Start-Up Visa program), and reliable legal support operations.

Our approach combines financial clarity, structured execution, and compliance-minded service delivery—ensuring every engagement is handled with professionalism, confidentiality, and accountability.
